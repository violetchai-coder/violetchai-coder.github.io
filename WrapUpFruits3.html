At Sol Bowl, we believe in the power of nature’s most vibrant ingredients. We source rare, exotic, and culturally significant fruits to bring you flavors you won't find anywhere else. Discover the unique taste of our tropical treasures below!

Gros Michel Banana

A richer, creamier banana with a floral aroma, once the king of bananas before the Cavendish.

Purple Passion Fruit

Tart and sweet with a fragrant pulp, native to South America but beloved worldwide.

Mangosteen

Known as the "Queen of Fruits," with a sweet, tangy white flesh and a hard purple rind.

Finger Lemon

An Australian citrus with tiny juice vesicles that burst like caviar, also called "Citrus Caviar."

Lychee

A juicy, translucent fruit with a floral sweetness, native to Southeast China.

Monstera Fruit

The delicious fruit of the Swiss Cheese plant, tasting like a mix between pineapple and banana.

Vanilla Cream Blood Orange

A unique citrus variety with a creamy texture and hints of vanilla alongside its signature berry flavor.

Pink Guava

Soft, sweet, and aromatic with a distinct musky fragrance and pink flesh.

Coconut Cream Mango

A tropical delight with the rich, creamy texture of coconut blended with sweet mango.
