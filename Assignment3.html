Albums

Ctrl 2017 Ctrl is the debut studio album by American singer SZA. The album explores themes of insecurity, failed relationships, and self-worth through a neo-soul and R&B lens. Featuring hits like "Love Galore" and "The Weekend", Ctrl was praised for its raw honesty and unique sound. It established SZA as a major voice in modern music, connecting with a generation navigating love and identity in the digital age.
SOS 2022 SOS is the highly anticipated second studio album by SZA. The album expands on her signature sound with a mix of R&B, pop, hip hop, and alternative rock influences. Tracks like "Good Days" and "I Hate U" showcase her emotional range and vulnerability. SOS is a cathartic project that deals with heartbreak, mental health, and personal growth, cementing her status as a leading artist of her generation.
SOS Deluxe: Lana 2024 SOS Deluxe: Lana is a reissue of the original SOS album, this expanded version adds seven new tracks to the original tracklist, including the popular single "Kill Bill". The deluxe edition explores themes of heartbreak, self-discovery, and female empowerment. It showcases SZA's unique blend of R&B, pop, and alternative sounds, offering fans a deeper look into her creative vision.
