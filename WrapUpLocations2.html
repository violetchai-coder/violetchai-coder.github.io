About Hana, Maui

Sol Bowl was born in Hana, Maui, a place surrounded by lush rainforests, black-sand beaches, and some of the rarest tropical fruits on Earth. Choosing Hana wasn’t just about location; it was about lifestyle. We wanted a home that reflects our values: sustainability, community, and deep respect for the land.

Being based in Hana allows us to work directly with local farmers, harvest unique fruits like monstera and finger lime, and share a taste of Hawaii with the world—one bowl at a time.
