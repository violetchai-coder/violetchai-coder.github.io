Spirited Away (千と千尋の神隠し)

Release Year:2001
Director:Hayao Miyazaki
Production Company:Studio Ghibli

Spirited Away follows the journey of Chihiro, a sullen 10-year-old girl who accidentally stumbles into a spirit world. Her parents are transformed into pigs as a result of consuming the spirits' food and Chihiro must work inside a magical bathhouse for Yubaba, a powerful witch, to free them and escape this non-human world. Through her trials and friendships, Chihiro matures, finds courage and identity, and ultimately breaks the spell that binds her family.

My Neighbor Totoro (となりのトトロ)

Release Year:1988
Director:Hayao Miyazaki
Production Company:Studio Ghibli

My Neighbor Totoro tells the story of Satsuki and Mei, two young sisters who move to the countryside with their father to be closer to their hospitalized mother. While exploring their new home, the girls discover small, magical forest spirits. Mei encounters a giant, friendly creature named Totoro and shares a wonderful adventure with him. The film is a heartwarming tale about childhood, nature, and the power of imagination.

Howl's Moving Castle (ハウルの動く城)

Release Year:2004
Director:Hayao Miyazaki
Production Company:Studio Ghibli

Sophie, a young hat maker, is cursed by the Witch of the Waste and transformed into a 90-year-old woman. Seeking a cure, she flees and finds refuge in the moving castle of the infamous wizard Howl. As Sophie cleans the messy castle, she learns about Howl's struggle against a futile war and his own fears. She forms an unlikely bond with Howl and his companions, which helps them both find freedom from their curses and the war that surrounds them.

Kiki's Delivery Service (魔女の宅急便)

Release Year:1989
Director:Hayao Miyazaki
Production Company:Studio Ghibli

Kiki's Delivery Service is about a young witch named Kiki who must leave home for a year to complete her training. She flies to a seaside city and decides to start a delivery service using her broomstick. At first, she struggles to adapt to her new life and faces a crisis of confidence when she loses her flying ability. With the help of her talking cat Jiji and new friends, Kiki learns to overcome self-doubt and find her place in the world.
