Sol Bowl is a small business rooted in Hana, Maui surrounded by lush rainforests, waterfalls, and black-sand beaches. We turn sustainability into something delicious through premium food, thoughtful service, and a top-tier experience always with our community and planet in mind. At the heart of our mission is quality and impact. We work to strengthen local development, support the well-being of rural communities across Hawaii, and help make healthy eating more accessible. Our goal is simple: bring local, cultural, and rare tropical fruits (many of which are too often overlooked) to our customers in a fresh, flavorful, and healthy way.

Our mission is to deliver quality with impact: strengthening local development, supporting rural communities across Hawaii, and making healthy eating more accessible. Sustainability shapes how we source and serve every bowl. We prioritize responsible practices that respect the land, support local growers and partners, and help protect the beauty that makes Hana and the islands so extraordinary. Through community relationships and rural co-ops, we ensure the harvest supports livelihoods, not just profits.
